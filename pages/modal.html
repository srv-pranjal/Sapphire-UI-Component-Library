Modal

A modal is a window that appears in front of app content to provide critical information or ask for a decision.

Simple Modal

A simple modal can be used to display short information and ask for user's interaction. This can be done by building a container with class name - modal wrapped in a container with class name - modal-wrapper.
Check out the Live Demo by clicking on the Demo button.

Modal Header

By clicking “Accept”, you agree this site can store cookies on your device and disclose information in accordance with our Cookie Policy. 

    <div class="modal-wrapper">
        <div class="modal">
            <div class="modal__header">
                <h2>Modal Header</h2>
                <a class="modal__btn-close">
                    <i class="fa fa-times-circle"></i>
                </a>
            </div>
            <hr />
            <div class="modal__body">
              By clicking “Accept”, you agree this site can store cookies on your device and disclose information in accordance with our Cookie Policy.
            </div>
            <div class="modal__footer">
                <button class="btn btn--outline-primary">Cancel</button>
                <button class="btn btn--primary">Accept</button>
            </div>
        </div>
    </div>

List Modal

A list modal display a list of items that take immediate effect when selected, for e.g. Selecting account after clicking on Login with google option.
Copy the below code snippet to get started with a List modal.

Set backup account

user01@gmail.com
user02@gmail.com
Add Account
